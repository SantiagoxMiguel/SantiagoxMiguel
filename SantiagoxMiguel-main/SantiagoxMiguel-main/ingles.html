ACTIVIDAD 2: LEER Y TRADUCIR EL VOCABULARIO DE ENFERMEDADES Y SÍNTOMAS QUE SE DA A CONTINUACIÓN.
VOCABULARY: SOME ILLNESSES AND SYMPTOMES
1. AN ALLERGY: A NEGATIVE REACTION T SOMETHING YOU EAT OR TOUCH.
2. ASTHMA: A SERIOUS RESPIRATORY PROBLEM. DIFFICUTY F BREATHING.
3. A BACKACHE: A PROLONGED PAIN IN THE BACK.
4. A BROKEN LEG: A BONE IN THE LEG IS BROKEN.
5. A COUGH: THE ACT OF EXPELLING AIR FROM THE LUNGS WITH A SUDDEN SHARP SOUND.
6. A COLD: A COMMON VIRAL INFECTION WICH CAUSES MUCUS TO RUN FROM THE NOSE. IT GIVES A SORE THROAT AND OFTEN INCLUDES SNEEZING.
7. AN EARACHE: A PAIN INSIDE THE EAR.
8. A FEVER: AN ABNORMALLY HIGH BODY TEMPERATURE USUALLY ACCOMPANIED BY SHIVERING AND HEADACHE.
9. THE FLU: IS THE COMMON NAME GIVEN FOR INFLUENZA. IT IS A CONTAGIUS VIRAL INFECTION OF THE RESPIRATORY PASSAGES THAT CAUSES FEVER AND SEVER ACHING.
10. A HEADACHE: A CONTINUOUS PAIN IN THE HEAD.
11. A HEARTBURN: A FORM OF INDIGESTION FELT AS A BURNING SENSATION IN THE CHEST.
12. A RASH: A LOT OF SMALL RED SPOTS ON THE SKIN THAT ARE USUALLY ITCHY.
13. A SORE THROAT: A CONDITION OF PAIN IN THE THROAT TIPICALY CAUSED BY INFLAMATION OF IT.
14. A SPRAIN: AN INJURY TO A JOINT IN YUR BODY ESPECIALLY YUR WRIST OR ANKLE CAUSED BY A SUDDEN TWISTING ON IT.
15. A STOMACHACHE: A PAIN IN THE PERSON’S BELLY CAUSED BY FOOD THAT DROPS BADLY.
16. A TOTHACHE: THE PAIN IN A TOOTH OR THEETH CAUSED BY CARIES OR FOR EATING VERY HARD FOOD.
